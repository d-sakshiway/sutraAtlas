Getting Started

Sutra-Atlas is a comprehensive Digital Learning Resource Management System (DL-RMS) designed to help you organize, manage, and track your educational resources effectively.

Key Features:

  • Collections: Organize resources by subject, project, or any category
  • Resource Management: Store and track books, articles, videos, and more
  • Status Tracking: Track your progress (Not Started, In Progress, Paused, Completed)
  • Search & Filter: Quickly find what you're looking for
  • Personal Dashboard: Your personalized learning workspace

New User
  1. Click "Register" in the navigation
  2. Create your account with email and secure password
  3. Log in to access your dashboard
  4. Create your first collection
  5. Start adding resources to your collections

System Requirements
  • Browser: Any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Internet: Stable internet connection
  • Account: Valid email address for registration
  • JavaScript: Must be enabled in your browser

No downloads or installations required! Sutra-Atlas is a web-based application that works directly in your browser.

Account & Security

Your Security is Our Priority
  • Password Security: All passwords are securely hashed and encrypted
  • Private Collections: Only you can access your collections and resources
  • Secure Sessions: Your login sessions are protected and automatically expire
  • Input Validation: All data is validated to prevent security vulnerabilities
  • Regular Backups: Your data is regularly backed up for protection

Strong Password Requirements

To keep your account secure, passwords must include:

  • At least 8 characters long
  • At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  • At least one number (0-9)

Example of a strong password: MySecure123
